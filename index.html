Who We Are

At YITH – Youth in the Heart – we specialize in physical and occupational therapy for seniors. Our mission is to restore mobility, reduce pain, and renew vitality through personalized, heart-centered care.

Whether recovering from surgery, managing arthritis, or simply seeking to stay active, our clients receive evidence-based treatment in a warm, welcoming environment.

Our Mission

We empower older adults to live with strength, dignity, and independence by delivering compassionate, evidence-based care through physical therapy, home visits, virtual visits, and in-clinic services. We assess, improve, and manage mobility, acute and chronic conditions, and create personalized environmental modifications that support safety and fall prevention. Through strong social connections and holistic support, we help every individual maintain independence and enhance their quality of life— wherever they call home.

Our Vision

To become Oklahoma City’s most trusted, heart-centered destination for geriatric rehabilitation and well-being—where older adults experience compassionate care, meaningful social connection, and personalized support that empowers them to live independently, safely, and joyfully at every stage of aging.
